⚽ Striker Run — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2026

Striker Run ("we", "our", or "the app") is a free hyper-casual mobile game developed by Giuseppe DiMalta. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use Striker Run on iOS.

Information We Collect

Striker Run itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Your high score is stored locally on your device only and is never sent to any server.

Advertising

Striker Run displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain information to serve ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (such as Apple's Identifier for Advertisers, IDFA)
  • IP address and general location (country/region)
  • Device type, operating system, and language
  • Ad interaction data (views, clicks)

This data is collected and processed by Google LLC in accordance with their own privacy policy. You can review Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time by going to Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking on your iPhone and disabling ad tracking.

App Tracking Transparency

On iOS 14 and later, Striker Run will ask for your permission before allowing AdMob to track your activity across other apps and websites for advertising purposes. You are free to decline this permission — the game works fully without it.

Children's Privacy

Striker Run is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The advertising displayed in the app is not targeted at children and does not use behavioral data for users who have not provided tracking consent.

Data Storage

The only data stored by the app is your best score, saved locally on your device using standard iOS storage (localStorage). This data never leaves your device and is deleted if you uninstall the app.

Third-Party Services

The only third-party service integrated into Striker Run is Google AdMob. We do not use analytics tools, crash reporting services, or any other third-party SDKs.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
